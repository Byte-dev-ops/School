At Fatehabad where the Society has decided to run educational institutes in urban area surrounding with 5 towns & 50 villages having population approximate 4 lacs, is a great need of an education college, elementary education institute, a polytechnic institute and engineering college in this area . ---Objectives---


Development of Fatehabad

The main motive of this school is to develop Fatehabad area which was considered as backward in the field of study. Now The area is getting its peak goals and Students of this collage are getting very praiseable posts as well as placements in various fields.

Quality Education

Quality Education is also the main motive of this school because no Institution can stand forever until it does not give quality in education. So no stone is left unturned to achieve the goal of quality education by the team of this school.All help provide to the poor students.

Cultural Programes

To organise cultural programmes and to promote and upliftment of cultural activities in the area and to do such activities to boost cultural heritage of Punjab and india.To provide Medical Facility,co-operation,mutual harmony and love and affection amont the members of society.
